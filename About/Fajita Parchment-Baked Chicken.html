Fajita Parchment-Baked Chicken

Fajita parchment-baked chicken is a delicious and flavorful dish that combines the bold and vibrant flavors of traditional fajitas with the convenience and health benefits of parchment baking. This culinary creation involves marinating chicken in a tantalizing blend of spices, including cumin, chili powder, garlic, and lime juice, to infuse it with a zesty kick. The marinated chicken is then placed on a bed of colorful bell peppers and onions, enhancing both the taste and visual appeal of the dish. Parchment baking ensures that the chicken remains moist and succulent, while also allowing the flavors to meld harmoniously. The result is a mouthwatering and aromatic meal that captures the essence of fajitas in a wholesome and easy-to-prepare package. Whether served with warm tortillas, rice, or a refreshing side salad, fajita parchment-baked chicken is a delightful culinary adventure that satisfies the taste buds and adds a touch of Tex-Mex flair to any dining experience.
